International Workshop on Discrete Mathematics and Algorithms 2026 (DMA2026)

March 16–18, 2026 · Kunibiki Messe, Matsue, Japan

International Workshop on Discrete Mathematics and Algorithms (DMA) is a workshop that brings together researchers in discrete mathematics and algorithms to share new ideas, receive friendly feedback, and build collaboration across regions. The 2026 meeting emphasizes lively discussion, early-stage results, and community building.

DMA2026 is supported by JSPS KAKENHI 22H00513, and the National Research Foundation of Korea (RS-2025-00523206).
